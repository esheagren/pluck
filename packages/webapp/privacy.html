Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 5, 2025

Pluckk ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our browser extension and web application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and basic profile information (name, profile picture) to create and manage your account.
  • Flashcard Data: The text you select and the flashcards generated from that text are stored to enable the review functionality.
  • Usage Data: We track the number of cards generated per month for billing purposes.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Pluckk service
  • To authenticate your account
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • To enforce usage limits for free tier accounts

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which provides enterprise-grade security including encryption at rest and in transit. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google: For authentication (OAuth)
  • Supabase: For data storage and authentication
  • Stripe: For payment processing
  • Anthropic (Claude): For AI-powered flashcard generation

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your flashcard data

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@pluckk.app.
